## About Me

I have always been fascinated by the logic behind the applications we use every day. This curiosity naturally led me to technology, where I discovered my passion for solving problems through code.

As a developer in training, my focus is on building a strong technical foundation. I don’t aim just to make things work; I aim to understand how and why they work. I have been applying my studies in practical projects that simulate real-world scenarios, working with React, Node.js, REST APIs, Git, and software development best practices.

Beyond technical skills, I value discipline, autonomy, and continuous learning. My portfolio projects reflect my commitment to turning theory into functional and well-structured applications.

My professional goal is to secure my first opportunity as a Web Developer in a collaborative environment, where I can contribute proactively, learn from experienced developers, and grow through real-world challenges. 

I am open to connections, knowledge sharing, and opportunities.
